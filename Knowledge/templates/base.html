How to Use Knowledge Graph Builder
What is a Knowledge Graph?

A knowledge graph represents information as a network of entities (nodes) and relationships (edges) between them, allowing for semantic connections and insights.

Steps to Create a Graph:
  1. Input your content - You can paste text, provide a URL, or upload a text file.
  2. Generate the graph - Our AI will extract entities and relationships from your content.
  3. Explore and analyze - Interact with the visualization, search for specific entities, and download your graph.
Features:
  • Interactive visualization - Zoom, drag, and hover over nodes for more information.
  • Download options - Save your graph as JSON, CSV, PNG, or SVG.
  • Search functionality - Find specific entities and relationships within your graph.
